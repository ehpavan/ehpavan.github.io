About Me

Hi, I'm Pavan — a 16-year-old cybersecurity enthusiast from India. My journey into hacking began early, driven by curiosity and a desire to understand how systems work under the hood.

Over the years, I’ve developed skills across Python, Bash, SQL, Red Teaming, API Security, Web Security, Linux, Mobile Security, and Active Directory, while actively participating in bug bounty programs and building automation for reconnaissance and testing.

I’m currently building ZeroClyne, a platform focused on continuous security, attack surface visibility, and real-world protection across web, cloud, and API environments.

My long-term goal is to become a next-generation security engineer, blending AI, automation, and deep technical research to make the internet safer.
